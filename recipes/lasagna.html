Lasagna

Description

To prepare lasagna from scratch, you start by making sauce, then layer it with wide, flat lasagna noodles and cheese before baking. The sauces can vary from tomato-based sauce, with or without meat, to creamy bechamel sauce, which works well with a wide variety of vegetables.

FOR THE SAUCE

  1. 1cup extra virgin olive oil
  2. 2medium red onions, finely diced
  3. 2large cloves minced garlic
  4. 8ounces pancetta, diced
  5. Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  6. 1½cups good red wine, preferably Italian
  7. 228-ounce cans Italian plum tomatoes
  8. 3tablespoons tomato paste
  9. ¾pound ground sirloin
  10. ¼cup freshly grated pecorino Romano
  11. 2eggs
  12. 10sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, washed and dried
  13. 2large whole cloves garlic
  14. ½cup flour
  15. 1pound Italian sausage, a mix of hot and sweet

FOR THE LASAGNA

  1. 115-ounce container ricotta cheese
  2. 2eggs
  3. 2cups freshly grated pecorino Romano
  4. ½cup chopped parsley
  5. 1pound mozzarella, grated
  6. 16sheets fresh lasagna noodles, preferably Antica Pasteria
